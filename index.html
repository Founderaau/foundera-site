What We Do

One platform.
Three paths.

The Launchpad

Every month, young Australian founders take the stage in front of investors, mentors and peers. Real feedback, real connections and a real shot at funding. Whatever stage you are at, this is where things accelerate. The investors are looking for someone worth backing. This is where ideas become something.

The Foundation

Real founders. Real stories. Real access. Every month we bring the people who have built something into a room with the people just getting started. Hear the journey, ask the hard questions and connect with others building the same way you are. The kind of room that turns an idea into something worth backing.

The Workshop

Most founders are great at building but struggle with everything around it. These sessions were built for that gap. Hands on, practical and focused on the skills that actually determine how far your company goes. From pitching and fundraising to product thinking and team building. No theory. Just the work.

Industry Panels

Every month we put you in the room with senior leaders from Australia's biggest firms. Small enough to actually talk to them. Hear how they built their careers, what their industries actually look like from the inside and what they wish someone had told them when they were exactly where you are now.

Career Mentoring

Paired with professionals in your exact field who have already walked the path you are on. One on one sessions that fast-track your career growth, sharpen your direction and give you honest advice from someone who genuinely remembers what it was like to be exactly where you are.

Skill Workshops

Your degree gets you to the door. These workshops get you through it. Practical, hands on sessions built around the skills universities do not teach. Resume building, interview prep, networking, negotiation and the professional habits that turn a good student into someone employers actually want to hire.

Campus Programs

We partner with high schools and universities across Australia to deliver programs that go beyond the classroom. Real mentoring, real career exposure and hands on skill building, all designed around what students actually need to land internships, build confidence and take the next step.

Student Mentoring

Your mentor was in your shoes not long ago. They know the path because they just walked it. Matched on your field and your goals, these one on one sessions give you a clear roadmap from someone who just came out the other side. We help you land it.

The Foundera Business Challenge

Case competitions, industry simulations and summits that put students in front of real business problems and real professionals. Teams compete, present and are judged by people who are actually hiring. Strong performances open doors. This is how students turn a degree into a career opportunity before graduation.
